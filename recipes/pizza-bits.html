Pizza Bites

Description

These are ooey and gooey, offering all the flavor of pizza but at a fraction of the labor and time it takes to make a full pizza. Being bite-size not only makes these a great grab-and-go snack for kids but also a fun party appetizer or game day snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 package Pillsbury Crescent Rolls , 8 oz
  • avocado oil spray, (optional)
  • 8 teaspoons pizza sauce
  • 8 pieces fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 8 slices pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Unroll the Pillsbury dough, pull apart each piece and take each triangle and roll it into a ball. Flatten that dough ball and spray your muffin tin with some avocado spray, if using. If your muffin tin is brand new and non-stick you might not have to spray it at all.
  3. Flatten each piece of dough into the muffin tin and make sure to press it down and along the sides so that it looks like a little cup
  4. Place 1 teaspoon of pizza sauce into each cup and top with a piece of mozzarella.
  5. Add a slice of pepperoni on top and bake for 15 minutes or until the pizza bites are puffed up and golden. Enjoy!
